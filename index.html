Our Story

Our story began not with a swipe right, but with a job interview. In what can only be described as the most successful screening call of all time, Alief (the candidate) first met Aulia (the HR) on March 3, 2021.


He got the job, and it was during an on-camera meeting a month later that a quiet spark was lit. That spark remained a well-kept secret for nearly three years (at least, that’s what Alief thought) until fate—with a little help from mutual friends—reconnected us over an impromptu dinner in January 2024.


From there, the connection blossomed, leading to a long-awaited memorable moment on 24.04.24, when Aulia finally received Alief’s confession. On June 1, 2025, with the heartfelt blessings of Aulia’s mother and brother, we committed to our forever.


Now, we are counting down the days until our ultimate partnership begins, as we get ready to build our most beautiful project: a warm and happy home together.
